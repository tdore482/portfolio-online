Design for Oasis Brands (UI/UX Design Process)

Oasis Brands is a forward-thinking company specializing in branding and design solutions. To enhance its digital presence, a strategic UI/UX approach was implemented to create an engaging and user-friendly experience. This case study explores the key design decisions made in crafting an immersive website for Oasis Brands.

Visual Design

  • Cut-Out Imagery & Logo-Type Elements: Used to reflect Oasis Brands' creativity and vision.

  • Color Palette & Typography: A curated Oasis color scheme with strategic orange accents.

  • Visual Harmony: Consistent style and typography reinforce brand identity.

Navigation & Layout

  • Progressive Disclosure: Animations in the "Our Services" section reveal content in stages.

  • Hover Interactions: Strategically placed to create a guided journey.

  • Structured Engagement: Each interaction encourages intuitive exploration.

Core Features

  • Animated Service Reveal: Keeps users engaged while presenting complex information gradually.

  • Interactive Hover Effects: Enhances user experience and interactivity.

  • Brand-Focused Visual Integration: Imagery and logo-type designs consistently align with the brand's creative mission.

Outcome & Impact

  • Users experienced fluid, engaging storytelling without cognitive overload.

  • The design reinforced Oasis Brands' image as a visionary, creative leader.

  • Increased engagement and smoother content absorption through interactive features.
