Passion & Purpose for Youth on Global Development

Brand Story & Web Presentation

The Goal

To visually and narratively present an organisation driven by youth empowerment—focused on education, self-development, mental health awareness, and equal opportunity for underrepresented students.

Visual Overview

Vision

Empowering youth to drive transformative change in education and health, ensuring every child has access to quality learning and holistic wellbeing.

Mission

To harness the passion and purpose of youth through innovative programs, partnerships, and advocacy—improving education, self-development, and health outcomes for Malawi and beyond.

Focus Areas

• Quality education access for girls & underrepresented students
• Youth self-development
• Ensuring every child learns under any circumstance
• Mental health awareness among children & youth

Our Story

Passion and Purpose for Youth on Global Development supports children and young people who feel underrepresented and disempowered. The organisation equips youth with confidence, skills, and academic empowerment, with a strong focus on emotional wellbeing. Their model targets students at transitional stages—from Form 4, to university entry, to early career development.


Their approach centres on collaboration: working with schools, universities, youth groups, and mentors to integrate essential skills into learning environments.

Founder

Founded by Agness Msango, an education and mental health activist with an MBA from Amity University and a BA in Procurement & Supply Chain Management. Coming from a low-income background, Agness struggled with access to quality educational resources—but overcame these barriers to graduate and become an advocate for youth development.


Her mission: to ensure young people receive the guidance and support she lacked during her Form 4 and university journey.
