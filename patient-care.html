Designing an Inclusive Telemedicine Interface for Patient Care

CLIENT

Patient Care

PROJECT TYPE

Web Design

The Patient Care platform was designed to provide a seamless and engaging telemedicine experience for patients, doctors, and partners. The goal was to create an intuitive, accessible, and visually appealing web interface.

Visual Design

  • Color Scheme: Soft, calming tones (purples, greens, whites) for a medical and trustworthy feel.
  • Typography: Clean, sans-serif fonts for readability.
  • Icons & Imagery: Intuitive visuals to aid quick understanding.

Navigation & Layout

  • Minimalist Structure: Clear hierarchy for easy navigation.
  • CTA Buttons: Prominent for booking and accessing records.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for all devices.

Core Features

  • User-Specific Portals: Simple access for patients, doctors, and partners.
  • Real-Time Booking & Alerts: Streamlined scheduling.
  • Accessibility: Features like voice commands and larger fonts.

Outcome & Impact

  • Increased patient-doctor interaction through intuitive design.
  • Improved accessibility for diverse users.
  • Strong brand identity and user trust.
