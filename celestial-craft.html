This case study analyzes the UI/UX design of Celestial Crafts' landing page, focusing on layout, typography, color scheme, usability, and accessibility to create a strong digital presence.

Visual Design

  • Hero Section: Bold uppercase headline, vibrant blue background for strong first impression.
  • Typography: Bold sans-serif for headings, clear hierarchy for readability.
  • Color Scheme: Primary vibrant blue (#00A3E0), black & white accents for contrast.

Navigation & Layout

  • About Section: Split layout for visual balance between imagery and text.
  • Services Section: Card-based design for easy scanning.
  • Footer: Minimalist, uncluttered, essential contact info only.

Core Features

  • CTA Placement: Strategically positioned for conversions.
  • Usability Enhancements: Visual hierarchy prevents fatigue.
  • Accessibility: High text-to-background contrast & intuitive visuals.

Outcome & Impact

  • Created a bold, engaging brand identity for Celestial Craft.
  • Improved user comprehension and interaction via clear layouts.
  • Established trust and credibility through a professional design approach.
