Sustainability & Profitability.

Client

North East Foods Ltd

Type

Web Design & Dev

The Concept

Showcasing farming as a sustainable and profitable business for Malawian farmers.

The goal was to build an informative platform that educates users on the mission of North East Foods while detailing their approaches and product ranges.

Project Gallery

Visual Design

A Green and White palette reflects the brand's nature-focused identity. Basic fonts paired with bold headers ensure a professional, modern appeal.

Core Features

Highly detailed product cards inform consumers of content. A strong narrative in the About Section highlights the organization's mission.

Navigation

A streamlined structure with clear hierarchy, allowing a storytelling flow from intro to about, approaches, and finally the product range.
