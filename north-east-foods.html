Informative website, Showcasing sustainability and profitability

CLIENT

North East Foods Limited

PROJECT TYPE

Web Design | Web Development

This website was created to showcase farming for sustainable and profitable business for Malawian farmers

Visual Design

  • Color Palette: Green and white which showcase the brands color pallete.
  • Typography: Basic fonts paired with bold headers for modern appeal and professionalism.
  • Imagery: High-quality images to showcase farming and processes to achieve North East Foods mission

Navigation & Layout

  • Streamlined Structure: Clear hierarchy for easy scanning.
  • Storytelling Flow: From intro to about, approaches, and product range.
  • Mobile Responsiveness: Optimized for all screen sizes.

Core Features

  • About Section: Strong narrative of organization.
  • Product Cards: Highly detailed yet organized product cards to inform consumer's of product's content.
  • Experience Timeline: Clear display of roles and achievements.
