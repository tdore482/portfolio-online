ABOUT

As a passionate UI/UX Designer, I specialize in crafting intuitive, engaging, and accessible digital experiences that bridge the gap between users and technology. With a background in human-computer interaction, frontend development, and information systems analysis, I blend creativity with functionality to design seamless user journeys.



MY DESIGN PROCESS

Discovery & Research

Every successful project starts with clarity.

I begin by understanding your goals, users, and the problem we’re solving. This includes reviewing your brand, audience, competitors, and market trends to shape a strategic foundation.


Wireframing & Sketching

Structure before style.

I sketch low-fidelity wireframes to plan layouts, functionality, and key interactions. This rapid ideation phase allows for fast feedback and iteration.

  • Wireframes (low-fidelity)
  • Screen layout sketches
  • UX flow diagrams

UI Design & Prototyping

Crafting beautiful, usable interfaces.

Once structure is solid, I apply visual design principles colors, typography, spacing, and branding to build engaging high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes.

  • Pixel-perfect UI mockups
  • Clickable prototypes
  • Design systems or style guides

User Testing & Validation

Design backed by real feedback.

I test the prototype with real users or stakeholders, gathering insights on usability and user experience. Then I refine the design to align with what truly works.

  • Usability testing
  • Iteration based on feedback
  • Updated prototypes

Handoff & Development Support

Making sure the design works in code.

I package design assets and specifications for developers and collaborate during the build to maintain quality and alignment.

  • Exported assets & icons
  • Handoff files via Figma or Zeplin
  • Dev support for pixel-perfect implementation
